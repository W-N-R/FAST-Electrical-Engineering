# FAST Electrical Engineering — Past Papers & Study Material

A curated archive of **FAST-NUCES Electrical Engineering** past papers and study
material, organized by discipline → category → course.

```
Electrical Engineering/
├── <Course>/
│   ├── Past Papers/            # exams (mid / final / quiz), one PDF per paper
│   └── Study Material/
│       ├── Notes/
│       ├── Labs & Manuals/
│       ├── Slides & Lectures/
│       ├── Course Outlines/
│       └── Books/
└── Reference Books/
```

- Scanned/photographed papers are auto-rotated and bundled into clean, named PDFs.
- Powers the **FAST ATM** app's Engineering department.

*Contributions welcome — add papers under the matching course's `Past Papers/` folder.*
